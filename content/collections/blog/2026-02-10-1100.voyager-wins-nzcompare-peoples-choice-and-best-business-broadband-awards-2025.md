Four years in a row - and it still means the world to us.

Voyager has once again been recognised at the NZCompare Awards, taking home People’s Choice Broadband and Best Business Broadband for 2025 and we couldn’t be more grateful.

Winning the People’s Choice Award for the fourth consecutive year is a huge honour. Because it’s entirely driven by customer votes, it reflects the real experiences people have with Voyager and our team every day. That’s what makes this award so meaningful.

Being awarded Best Business Broadband by the NZCompare judges is another major milestone. After several years as a finalist, this win recognises our commitment to helping Kiwi businesses thrive. From startups to enterprises, we’ve delivered tailored connectivity, cloud, communication, and security solutions, supported by dedicated consultants and partner managers who are with our customers every step of the way.

Customer Experience at the Core of Everything We Do
What connects both awards is our foundation: exceptional customer support. Since Voyager launched, our focus has been simple: deliver dependable broadband and technology services, while putting customers first. Our team genuinely cares and that’s what our customers keep telling us makes the difference. Whether we’re connecting homes, powering businesses, or supporting partners, our focus remains the same: do things properly, and do them well.

We believe great broadband is about more than just speed. It’s about people. Knowledgeable, timely support from a team who’s got your back is what transforms a good experience into a great one and in turn, sets Voyager apart.

Looking Ahead
Awards like these offer a chance to reflect. In recent years and with past NZCompare wins as a signal we’re on the right track, we’ve continued to invest in our network, expand our offerings, and refine how we support customers.

With that in mind, these awards are never a finish line. In 2026, we’re doubling down on what matters most: better performance, smarter tools and hardware, increased reliability, and support that continues to show up when you need it. These awards confirm our direction - and push us to keep raising the bar.

Grateful for the People Who Make Voyager What It Is
Finally, we want to take a moment to say thank you. These awards are only possible because of the people behind Voyager and the customers who continue to place their trust in us. To our team, whose dedication and care show up in every customer interaction, and to our customers, whose loyalty continues to drive us forward - these awards belong to you as much as they do to Voyager.

“We might not be the biggest provider, but we’re proud to have one of the best networks, and an even better team behind it. Our customers value high-quality connectivity, combined with a team of people who really care, and we’re grateful for every one of them. We’re proud to be their ISP of choice, as without their support and votes, we wouldn’t be in to win these awards.”

- Seeby Woodhouse, Founder and CEO of Voyager.

Thank you again to everyone who voted, supported us, and continues to choose Voyager. We’re proud to keep connecting Kiwis to what matters most.
